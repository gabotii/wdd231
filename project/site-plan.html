WDD 231 Final Project Site Plan - Gabriel Torrejon

Site Name

Sublima lab - Customizing t-shirts, mugs and much more

Project Description The website Sublima lab will serve as an engaging and user-friendly platform for individuals and businesses seeking personalized printing services. Visitors can browse a wide range of customizable products such as T-shirts, mugs, and accessories. The site will include an innovative online customization tool that allows users to design their own items, offering options for colors, text, images, and more. Additionally, customers can explore detailed information about available printing techniques, pricing, delivery options, and bulk order services. This website not only facilitates product creation but also showcases the business's commitment to creativity, quality, and customer satisfaction. The goal is to deliver a seamless experience for customers to create one-of-a-kind products for personal use, gifts, or promotional purposes. The website Sublima lab is dedicated to providing a seamless platform for individuals and businesses to create and purchase personalized products. The content of the website includes: A comprehensive catalog of customizable items such as T-shirts, mugs, and accessories. An intuitive online customization tool enabling users to design their own products by selecting colors, adding text, and uploading images. Informative pages explaining various printing techniques like screen printing and sublimation. Detailed pricing information and flexible delivery options tailored for individual and bulk orders. A contact page to address customer queries and handle special requests for large-scale events. This site ensures visitors can access all necessary information while enjoying a smooth and interactive user experience. It emphasizes user-friendliness, showcasing the creativity and quality of Sublima lab's offerings. The website content is structured to provide clarity and engagement for customers, whether they want to design gifts, order promotional items, or explore the printing process.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to empower customers to express their individuality through customized products while providing a reliable platform for bulk orders and business needs. By centralizing services into an interactive and informative site, Sublima lab aims to: Increase Customer Engagement: Offer a fun and straightforward design tool that makes product customization enjoyable and accessible. Expand Reach: Attract a broader audience by showcasing Sublima lab's expertise in both personal and business customization services. Educate Visitors: Provide detailed information on printing techniques, enabling customers to make informed decisions about their purchases. Simplify Bulk Ordering: Facilitate bulk orders for businesses and events, emphasizing professionalism and convenience. Foster Creativity: Inspire users to create unique, personalized items, fostering a sense of ownership and creativity. This project reflects the importance of bridging creativity and commerce, providing a platform that seamlessly blends design, information, and functionality. Through Sublima lab, customers can experience an easy, fun, and rewarding way to create custom products tailored to their needs. Sublima lab serves as a comprehensive platform for customers to:

  • Browse and order customized T-shirts, mugs, and other items.
  • Design their own products using an online customization tool.
  • Learn about the different printing techniques available.
  • Access information on pricing and delivery options.
  • Contact the business for bulk orders and special requests.

The site aims to provide an easy and engaging way for customers to create unique, personalized products for themselves or as gifts.

Scenarios

  1. How can I design and order a custom T-shirt with my own artwork?
  2. What are the different printing techniques offered by Sublima lab?
  3. How do I place a bulk order for customized mugs for an event?

Color Schema

Primary Color
Secondary Color
Text Color
Warning Color
Faded Color

The primary color (hsl(212, 89%, 21%)) will be used for main headings and interactive elements like buttons.

The secondary color (hsl(0, 0%, 98%)) serves as the background color for the site, providing a clean and neutral base.

The text color (hsl(0, 0%, 20%)) ensures good readability against the light background.

Typography

Headings: Roboto, sans-serif

Body Text: Open Sans, sans-serif

This combination provides a modern look while ensuring readability across devices.
